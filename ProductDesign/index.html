Kahoot color choice, and readability

Both in my role as a teacher and as a student, I observed the challenge of reading white text on a yellow background when using a projector. To address this issue, I reached out to Kahoot for consultation.

Their white text on yellow background was against their own design guidlines. There hasn't been much testing of their software within an actual classroom setting, despite this being their primary target environment for their customer engagement.
